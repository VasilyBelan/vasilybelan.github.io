From Google's domain, I chose WeathersMood.com which was priced at $12/year.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to indicate the exact weather of the audience. Information must be accurate and timely so that the client can navigate the weather and build their plans correctly. Also, the client should want to return to the site next time, which means the site should be attractive to the eyes, well-read, easy to navigate.
